RAAMable is a mashup of Google Maps with waypoint data of the 2009 route of the Race Across America and the location of nearby NOAA weather radio towers.

There are notes about the development on a TiddlyWeb hosted TiddlyWiki.

The code is freely available on GitHub.

See the credits for information on who did this.

The interactive map provides the following functionality (so far):

  • Displays the route on the map. Zooming the map zooms the route.
  • Display an elevation profile of the viewable portion of the route.
  • Shows the turns on the route.
  • Shows the time stations on the route.
  • Shows nearby NOAA radio towers, displaying their frequency on mouseover.

There's more to be done. See the wiki for notes about other ideas. Contact the developers if you have ideas, comments or questions.
